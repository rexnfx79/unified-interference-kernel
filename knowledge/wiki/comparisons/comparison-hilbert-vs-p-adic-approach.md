# Hilbert vs p-adic Approach

Side-by-side comparison for **connecting math to reality** in this project.

| Dimension | Hilbert / complex QM | p-adic / adelic |
|-----------|---------------------|-----------------|
| **SM QED foundation** | Standard | Non-standard; research programs only |
| **Inner product / probability** | Born rule established | Requires non-archimedean measure theory |
| **Flavor hierarchy** | Split fermions, overlaps | Ultrametric tree intuition |
| **Link to zeta** | Hilbert–Polya (operator on \(\mathbb{C}\)) | Adelic product formulas |
| **Testability** | Collider, precision QED | Unclear new predictions |
| **Plausibility (this wiki)** | **pursue** | **deprioritize** |
| **Best use** | QED → info → effective flavor | Watch for adelic boundary conditions |
