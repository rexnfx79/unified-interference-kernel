# Prime Numbers and Physics

## Proven

- Euler product and explicit formulas linking primes ‚Üî [[riemann-zeta-function]] (analytic number theory).

## Conjecture

- Primes enter physics via hypothetical spectral operator ([[hilbert-polya-conjecture]], [[primes-via-quantum-effects]]).

## Refuted / dead

- Direct prime indexing of SM mass ratios; zeta‚Üíflavor without mechanism ([[why-not-zeta-flavor-numerology]]).

## Why This Island

Primes are the atomic units of arithmetic. If math connects to reality, primes should enter **somewhere** ‚Äî but *where* and *how* is contested. This page separates established hooks from numerology.

## Established Connections

| Link | Mechanism | Strength |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Primes ‚Üî \(\zeta(s)\) | Euler product \(\zeta(s)=\prod_p(1-p^{-s})^{-1}\) | **Exact** (analytic) |

| Primes ‚Üî quantum chaos | **Indirect** via zeta zeros ‚Üî hypothetical spectrum | Conjectural (needs Hilbert‚ÄìPolya) |

## Quantum Routes (See [[primes-via-quantum-effects]])

1. **Spectral:** If zeros are eigenvalues, prime distribution is spectral density in disguise
2. **Semiclassical:** Berry‚ÄìKeating type \(H=xp\) ‚Äî primes in periodic orbit lengths (heuristic)
3. **Information:** Prime factorization hardness ‚Üî computational/thermodynamic bounds (QC, not SM masses)

## What Is **Not** Plausible

- Individual SM mass ratios encoding prime indices (e.g. \(m_\mu/m_e \approx p_k\)) ‚Äî **no QED derivation**, fits are post-hoc
- "Prime fields" replacing gauge fields without renormalizable Lagrangian ‚Äî **dead** until explicit EFT shown
