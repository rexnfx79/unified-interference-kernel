# Information Creates Physical Reality

## Core Claim

Physical reality is not independent of information — it **is** the stable, observable projection of constrained informational structure. "Matter" and "couplings" are readouts; the substrate is relational, spectral, or algorithmic information under compression.

## Honest Status (2026-06-02)

**No operational mechanism found in repo tests.** Diagnostics 12–19 exhaust pre-registered QIT→flavor routes:

- Post-hoc \(S(\rho_Y)\), QFI, decoherence proxies → **refuted** (12–16)
- Experimental PDG Fisher cross-sector alignment → **0.46** (below 0.50 threshold)
- Open-system pooled mixing → **refuted** (18)
- Fisher transfer quark→lepton → **refuted** (19: frozen loss 805.8, alignment 0.41)

The umbrella thesis remains **philosophical / speculative**. The interference kernel is a **phenomenological readout**, not evidence that information geometry derives flavor.
