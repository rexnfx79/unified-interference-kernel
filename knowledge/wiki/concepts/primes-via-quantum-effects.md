# Primes via Quantum Effects

## Thesis (Qualified)

Primes may enter physics **only where quantum theory meets discrete spectra or information bounds** — not as arbitrary labels on particles.

## Plausible Mechanisms

### 1. Quantization → discrete spectrum → arithmetic

Semiclassical quantization assigns energy levels; if those levels correlate with zero heights, prime distribution appears in **density of states**. Requires [[hilbert-polya-conjecture]] — still open.

### 2. Quantum information / it-from-bit

[[it-from-bit]]: measurement outcomes are bits. **Prime factorization** is informationally hard (Shor). Connection to *dynamics* is via quantum computation and entropy, not static mass ratios.

**Bridge to QED:** Feynman diagrams encode perturbative information; loop sums are not prime-indexed but **may** be constrained by holographic entropy bounds ([[quantum-information]]).

### 3. Quantum chaos

Closed orbits in chaotic systems have lengths that can be related to arithmetic in special billiards. Generic QED does not produce prime billiards.

## Implausible Mechanisms

| Claim | Problem |
|-------|---------|
| Electron "feels" primality | No operator in SM |
| Prime gaps → mass gaps | No mechanism; numerology |
| 3 generations because 3 is prime | Small integer coincidence |
