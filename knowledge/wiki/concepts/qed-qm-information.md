# QED and QM as Information Source

## Proven

- QED predictions tested to high precision; path-integral and Hilbert-space formulations equivalent for purposes of this wiki.
- S-matrix, anomalies, decoherence link to [[quantum-information]] measures (established QFT/QIT).

## Conjecture

- Envelope × phase EFT for Yukawas emerges from integrating out localized modes ([[split-fermion-localization]]).
- Information bounds constrain generation count or texture — not computed in repo.

## Not proven here

- Primes or zeta in QED loops without extra structure ([[can-primes-enter-via-qed-spectral-sums]]).
- [[it-from-bit]] ontology from QED alone.

## Strategy: Physics → Information → (Maybe) Arithmetic

The **most grounded** direction in this wiki is **not** arithmetic → flavor, but:

**Established QED/QM → rigorous information measures → constraints on what couplings/structures are possible**

Only *then* ask whether arithmetic objects appear in those constraints.

## What QED/QM Give Us (Established)

| Object | Information content |
|--------|---------------------|
| S-matrix | Transition amplitudes — "what can happen" |
| Path integral \(\int \mathcal{D}A\,\ e^{iS}\) | Sum over histories — phase interference |
| Renormalization | Scale-dependent information; loss of UV details (cf. [[projection-regimes]]) |
| Anomalies | Consistency constraints on chiral information |
| Decoherence | Environment-induced superselection — **it-from-bit** operationally |

## Path to Flavor (Without Numerology)

1. **Extra dimensions / localization:** Split fermions — wavefunction overlaps on [[hilbert-spaces-qm]] → Yukawa textures
2. **Effective field theory:** Integrate out heavy modes → suppressed operators matching envelope × phase ([[interference-kernel]] as **EFT ansatz**)
3. **Information bounds:** Bekenstein + unitarity → limits on degrees of freedom in volume — may constrain **number of generations** (speculative)

## What QED Does **Not** Give (Without New Physics)

- Riemann zeros directly
- Prime-indexed coupling constants
- Unique Yukawa matrix from QED alone — need flavor extension (SM Yukawa sector is empirical input)
