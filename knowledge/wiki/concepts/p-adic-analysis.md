# p-adic Analysis

## Definition

For prime \(p\), the **p-adic numbers** \(\mathbb{Q}_p\) complete \(\mathbb{Q}\) with ultrametric norm \(|x|_p = p^{-\nu_p(x)}\). Unlike \(\mathbb{R}\), ultrametric: every triangle is isosceles — **hierarchical** geometry.

## Why Consider It

| Potential fit | Concern |
|---------------|---------|
| Mass **hierarchy** (ultrametric tree) | Envelope suppression in [[interference-kernel]] already produces hierarchy on \(\mathbb{R}\) |
| **Adelic** physics — unify all completions | Volovich, etc.; elegant but few SM predictions |
| p-adic strings (historical) | Mostly abandoned for collider phenomenology |
| p-adic quantum mechanics | Non-archimedean Hilbert spaces — different from SM Hilbert space |

## Plausibility Verdict
