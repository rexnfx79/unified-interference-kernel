# Spectral Interpretation of Flavor

## Verdict: **deprioritized**

Direct identification of flavor with Hilbert–Polya / zeta spectra is **implausible** ([[why-not-zeta-flavor-numerology]]). A **narrower** spectral story remains open.

## Viable Sub-path (Pursue Instead)

**Split fermions on Hilbert space** ([[hilbert-spaces-qm]]):

1. Fermions localized in extra dimension
2. Overlap integrals \(\int \psi_L^* \psi_R \, dy\) produce Yukawa magnitudes
3. Phases from Wilson lines or extra-dimensional momentum — compare to \(\Phi_{ij}\) in [[interference-kernel]]
4. Derived from [[qed-qm-information]] / EFT, **not** from zeta zeros

## Failed Sub-path (Dead)

- Identify 3×3 Yukawa eigenvalues with first three zeta zeros
- Use 3×3 GUE spacing test ([[zeta-zero-spacing-yukawa-structure]] — **dead**)

## Constraint from Repo

[[repo-scientific-findings]]: parameters don't transfer across sectors. Any spectral story must predict **why** \(k_e \neq k_q\) etc., not post-label as "projection."

## Upgrade Criteria

Move from **deprioritized** → **pursue** if:

- Analytic derivation of [[interference-kernel]] from 1D Schrödinger overlaps
- One sector parameter relation predicted before fit
