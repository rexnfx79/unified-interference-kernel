# Chiral Projection Thesis

## Sharpened thesis (Tier A + B)

**Tier A (standard BSM):** Weak chirality is a property of our low-energy sector, not all of reality. A parity-symmetric parent theory may contain a paired sector with mirrored weak gauge assignment. Global parity is restored; local parity violation is sector-internal.

**Tier B (distinctive projection claim):** Which chiral EFT we inhabit may be fixed by orientation data \(\Omega\) of a reconstruction map \(\Pi_\Omega\) on a parent state \(\mathcal{C}\):

\[
\mathcal{C} \xrightarrow{\;\Pi_\Omega\;} \mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{SM}}, \qquad
\mathcal{C} \xrightarrow{\;\Pi_{-\Omega}\;} \mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{mirror}}
\]

Empirical content rests on **portal couplings** (gravity, neutrinos, photon kinetic mixing, nucleon oscillations). Holography and CPT-symmetric cosmology are **related but separate** programs.

## What this is NOT

| Confusion | Correction |
|-----------|------------|
| [[projection-regimes]] = chiral universe branches | Regimes are sector readouts **within** one chiral sector |
| Kernel non-universality = mirror evidence | Refuted transfer test; EFT/localization suffices |
| Holographic metaphor = proved mirror sector | AdS/CFT is specific; our universe is not AdS |
| Anomaly cancellation **requires** mirror world | Constrains content; does not mandate cosmological partner |
