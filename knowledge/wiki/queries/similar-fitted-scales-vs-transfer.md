# Similar Fitted Scales vs Parameter Transfer

## The confusion

`BOUNDARY_ANALYSIS_REPORT.md` reports σ ≈ 4–5 and α ≈ 2.5–3.0 **across sectors** when each sector is fit separately.

[[repo-scientific-findings]] **refutes** freezing quark parameters and applying to leptons (loss 797.5 vs 779.0).

These are **not contradictory** if stated precisely:

| Statement | Meaning | Status |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Similar **independently fitted** σ, α | Optimization finds comparable scales per sector | **phenomenological** observation — may be coincidence or shared geometry class |
| **Universal parameters** (freeze & transfer) | Same numeric σ*, k*, α*, η* work across sectors | **refuted** |

## Adversarial rule

Never cite boundary commonalities as evidence for universality without transfer test. Correlation of fit parameters ≠ predictive theory.
