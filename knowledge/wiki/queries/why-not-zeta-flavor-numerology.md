# Why Not Zeta–Flavor Numerology?

## The Trap

Both Riemann zeros and chaotic spectra show **GUE-like level repulsion**. It is tempting to conclude:

> "Flavor matrices are secretly zeta spectra → information creates reality."

This wiki **rejects** that jump unless a mechanism survives the checks below.

## Kill Criteria (Any One Fails → Bridge Dead)

1. **Scale:** Zeta: infinitely many zeros; flavor: 3×3 matrices. Need embedding \(3 \ll \infty\) with **derivation**, not analogy.
2. **Universality:** GUE appears in random matrices with **no physics**. Shared statistic ≠ shared cause.
3. **Predictivity:** Has the bridge predicted a **new** mass or mixing angle before measurement?
4. **Transfer:** [[repo-scientific-findings]] — quark parameters don't transfer to leptons. A zeta-spectrum story must explain **sector failure**, not ignore it.
5. **Operator:** Without explicit \(H\), Hilbert–Polya is a wish, not a bridge.

## What Would Revive the Bridge

- Construct \(H\) whose **first three** eigenvalue ratios match mass ratios **and** predict a fourth observable
- Derive CKM phase from spectral phase of \(H\) — not fit
- Show zeta zero **corrections** to a QED observable (any observable)

Until then: verdict **dead** for direct zeta→flavor in [[plausibility-register]].

## Safer Alternative Path

Arithmetic → (Hilbert–Polya) → **abstract** spectrum → **QFT embedding** → overlaps → flavor. Each arrow needs its own evidence. See [[multi-sided-bridge-framework]].
