# Adversarial Review — Chiral Projection Thesis

**Scope:** Speculation that observable spacetime is one oriented reconstruction of a deeper parity-symmetric structure, with an opposite-chirality mirror sector mostly decoupled from us.

**Verdict:** **Philosophically fertile, empirically uncommitted.** Survives only after stripping holographic overreach, flavor-bridge creep, and category conflations.

## 1. Fatal category errors

### Handedness ≠ weak chirality

Mirror reversal flips spatial parity. SM weak chirality is **gauge representation** (SU(2)\_L doublets vs singlets). Mirror sector needs duplicate gauge structure, not reversed image.

### Parity (P) ≠ CPT

CPT partner cosmology addresses charge+parity+time across Big Bang. This thesis is parity/mirror-sector. Merging them produces unfalsifiable mush.

### Holography ≠ mirror universe

AdS/CFT is proved in specific backgrounds. Our universe is approximately dS/LCDM. Boundary encoding does not imply opposite-chirality twin bulk without specified boundary theory and reconstruction map.

### Projection regimes ≠ chiral projection

Repo three regimes are sector-local Yukawa readouts. Diagnostics 12–16 **refuted** information-measure correlation with regime labels. Parameter non-transfer does not require mirror sector.

## 2. Explanatory limits

Even if true, the thesis does **not** explain:

- Three generations
- CKM / PMNS structure
- Quark CKM–\(m_c\) trade-off
- Right-handed neutrino elusiveness (without specified portal)

It explains **local parity violation given a hidden sector** — mirror matter already says that. The distinctive addition is **projection geometry**, currently unformalized at flavor level.
