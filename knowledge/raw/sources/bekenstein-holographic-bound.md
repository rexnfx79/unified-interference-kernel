# Bekenstein Holographic Bound (Curated Summary)

> **Primary source:** Bekenstein, "Universal upper bound on the entropy-to-energy ratio" (1981); Bekenstein, "Entropy bounds and the second law for black holes" (1982).  
> **Wiki concept:** [[holographic-principle]]

## Universal bound (established)

For a system of energy \(E\) and characteristic radius \(R\) in a weakly gravitating regime:

\[
S \leq \frac{2\pi k R E}{\hbar c}
\]

Entropy is bounded by **energy × size**, not volume — information scales with area at the holographic limit.

## Black hole entropy (established)

\[
S_{\mathrm{BH}} = \frac{k c^3 A}{4 G \hbar} = \frac{A}{4 \ell_P^2}
\]

Entropy of a black hole is proportional to **horizon area** \(A\), not volume.
