# Project Purpose

## Goal

Connect **mathematics to measurable reality** by approaching from multiple sides — arithmetic (primes, zeta, p-adics), spectral (Hilbert spaces, operators), and quantum/information (QED, QM, it-from-bit) — and document **where bridges hold**, **where they fail**, and **what is implausible**.

This is a **conjecture notebook**, not a proof archive. Every bridge must survive the gap-crossing protocol in [[multi-sided-bridge-framework]] and appear in [[plausibility-register]].
